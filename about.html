The Cafe

Step into a blocky sanctuary!

At MineCafe, we’ve crafted a space where the Overworld’s whimsy meets real-world comfort. Picture glowstone lanterns casting a warm light over wooden tables, potted nether wart accents, and walls lined with pixel art of your favorite mobs. Whether you’re here to refuel after a mining session or trade village gossip with friends, our café is your spawn point for relaxation.

Don’t forget to check the secret menu—sometimes, we serve cake (and yes, it’s always a lie).

Our Menu

  • Our Experience

  • The Founders Meet (2010)

    Two Minecraft-loving chefs crossed paths at a Manila gaming convention. Bonding over golden carrots and dreams of a block-themed café, they mined their first business plan on napkins at a local computer shop.

  • Create A Restaurant (2012)

    Our first MineCafe opened in Bonifacio Global City, serving pixel-shaped pancakes and 'potions' in glowstone jars. The line stretched longer than a Nether tunnel on opening day!

  • Added 200+ Employees (2015)

    From servers dressed as villagers to baristas trained in 'redstone brewing,' our team grew faster than bamboo in a jungle biome. All trained at our Pasig City headquarters.


  • Going Global (Philippine Roots)

  • Established Restaurant in Europe (2018)

    While opening in Berlin, we kept our Philippine heart - importing ube for our 'Purple Enderman Tart' and training all chefs in traditional Filipino hospitality.

  • Franchise Restaurants Brooklyn (2020)

    Our New York outpost features a 'Jollibee-Meets-Minecraft' breakfast combo, celebrating both our Philippine heritage and blocky creativity.

  • Added 100K More Employees (2022)

    Though now worldwide, 40% of our global staff are trained in Manila, spreading Filipino warmth through every 'Salamat po!' to customers.

  • Established Marketing (2023)

    Our Makati-based marketing team launched the viral 'Adobo vs. Creepers' campaign, proving Philippine flavors can conquer any biome!"
